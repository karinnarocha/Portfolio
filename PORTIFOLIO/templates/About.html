About
Hi! My name is Karinna , I'm a problem solver but also a maker.
A lover of technology and a logical thinker, I come from an architecture background where I had the pleasure of working for two years. I discovered that architecture wasn't my passion, and I wanted more. I moved to America for a new challenge in my life and immersed myself in a Computer Information Systems college. I became the first person in my family to earn an American college degree and discover new skills. I was solving problems, and my past career in architecture helped me get where I am today. I took advantage of learning Python, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React Native, and implemented my design mindset into my software development. My logical thinking allowed me to find the most efficient ways to complete tasks. Everything I have done, small or big, has been a vital stepping stone to where I am today.

Known as NurVIX on the internet, I started my streaming channel at the beginning of COVID-19. What began as a hobby became much more. I combined my passion for tech with my love of making connections. I started playing video games and ended up splitting my time coding with my community, where we all share knowledge and push each other forward. With a lot of hard work and time invested, I grew a channel that now reaches 3,500 followers and was fortunate enough to build an awesome and supportive community worldwide.

As a Jiu-jitsu lover, aside from technology, I have a passion for martial arts. It is a part of me where I learn every day to be humble, respectful, and grateful. It teaches me resilience, efficiency, and critical thinking, which I constantly implement in my daily life.
