Lulu&Remi

Lulu & Remi is a children’s creative brand built around interactive coloring–sticker books designed to spark imagination, hands-on learning, and joyful play. My responsibility was to design and develop a website that captured the brand’s playful identity while staying clean, modern, and optimized for parents looking for educational products.

From UI/UX design to front-end development, I created a fully responsive website that highlights the books, showcases the characters, and delivers a smooth shopping experience. The site is built with performance, visual appeal, and brand storytelling in mind — helping increase engagement, build trust with parents, and support future product launches.

The result is a vibrant yet simple digital experience that reflects the heart of the Lulu & Remi brand: creativity, fun, and learning.
